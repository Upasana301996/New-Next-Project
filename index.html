NEXT is a learning platform for Humber students to collaborate across programs, work directly with clients, and learn how to manage and market an agency. Here at NEXT, we value education, innovation, and the ability to think big. We know incredible work is being done by our students, so we want to showcase their talents while offering them real world experience. The next generation of creatives is here.


Building F is an initiative from the Faculty of Media and Creative Arts at Humber College, formerly known as The Ad centre, that has been running since 2016. The idea behind the program is to invite external organizations that have a creative challenge or project in the fields of marketing, advertising, publics relations, digital communications, and or design, to work with our students. Depending on the needs of the client, we connect them to the appropriate program and respective professors to bring the work into the classroom through a related assignment.  The aim is to create a more timely, relevant, and powerful educational experience, by giving students real work experience that they can leverage towards their chosen career.

A MESSAGE FROM THE CEO

To expand the program, whereas to create a student run agency that emulates a real agency and overseas the operations of the program. Students would be chosen from all related programs through an application process and work voluntarily 1 hour a week on projects and gain mentorship and guidance from an industry board and or faculty. The work would revolve around marketing, operations, client relations, and communications. In adding this new platform for students, we aim to give these students a competitive advantage by providing relevant agency experience and an environment to apply their learnings in a collaborative setting.
